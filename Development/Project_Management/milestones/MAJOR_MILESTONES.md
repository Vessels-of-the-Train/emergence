## Milestone Components

Each milestone includes:
- **Clear Deliverables**: Specific, measurable outcomes
- **Success Criteria**: How to verify completion
- **Dependencies**: What must be completed first
- **Timeline**: Target completion dates
- **Resources**: Required tools and support
- **Risk Assessment**: Potential challenges and mitigations

## Tracking Methodology

### Progress Indicators
- **Not Started**: Planning phase
- **In Progress**: Active development
- **Review**: Quality assurance phase
- **Complete**: Fully implemented and tested

### Regular Reviews
- **Weekly**: Component progress check
- **Monthly**: Milestone assessment
- **Quarterly**: Major milestone evaluation

## Milestone Achievement Process

### Before Milestone
1. **Planning**: Define clear deliverables and criteria
2. **Preparation**: Ensure all dependencies are met
3. **Resource Check**: Verify required tools and support

### During Milestone
1. **Execution**: Implement according to plan
2. **Tracking**: Monitor progress and adjust as needed
3. **Communication**: Keep stakeholders informed

### After Milestone
1. **Validation**: Confirm all criteria are met
2. **Documentation**: Record lessons learned
3. **Celebration**: Acknowledge achievement
4. **Transition**: Prepare for next phase
