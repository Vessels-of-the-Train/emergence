## Daily Writing Practice

### Morning Routine (30 minutes)
1. Review yesterday's work
2. Set today's specific goals
3. 10-minute free writing warm-up

### Writing Session (2-3 hours)
1. Review outline and character notes
2. Write new material or revise existing work
3. Track progress in writing log

### Evening Review (15 minutes)
1. Note what worked well
2. Identify challenges or blocks
3. Plan for next day's session

## Troubleshooting

### Writer's Block Solutions
1. **Free writing**: Write anything for 10 minutes
2. **Character interviews**: Interview your characters
3. **Scene prompts**: Use Aetherium prompts for scene ideas
4. **Research dive**: Explore consciousness research for inspiration

### Motivation Challenges
1. **Review progress**: Look at how far you've come
2. **Set micro-goals**: Break large tasks into small steps
3. **Connect with purpose**: Remember why you're writing this series
4. **Take breaks**: Sometimes stepping away helps
